Videos About GYM

A gym, short for gymnasium ( pl. : gymnasiums or gymnasia), is an indoor venue for exercise and sports. The word is derived from the ancient Greek term "gymnasion".

They are commonly found in athletic and fitness centres, and as activity and learning spaces in educational institutions.

Working out in a group can improve athletic performance. There's a reason that group fitness always makes it to some of the top fitness trends and most popular workout routines year on year. That's because working out in a group has proven health benefits on performance and motivation.
